🍎 macOS: how to open an app from an unidentified developer
  1. Open the .dmg and drag Warp to your Applications folder.
  2. Try to open Warp — macOS will block it with a warning.
  3. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security.
  4. Scroll down — you'll see "Warp was blocked" with an Open Anyway button.
  5. Click Open Anyway, then confirm in the prompt.
